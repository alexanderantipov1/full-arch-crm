# Insurance Issue

## Profile Summary
Patient with active insurance who has encountered a claim denial, prior auth delay, or eligibility confusion. High frustration level. At-risk for leaving the practice. Quick, transparent resolution retains 83% of these patients.

## Common Issue Types
1. **Prior auth pending >14 days** — patient cancels surgery appointment
2. **Claim denied** — patient gets unexpected bill
3. **Eligibility mismatch** — patient thought they were covered, weren't
4. **Frequency limitation** — procedure done too recently per payer rules

## Resolution Intelligence
- Call payer same day denial is received — don't wait for patient to call first
- Always have appeal template ready (see [[insurance/appeals/]])
- For prior auth >14 days: escalate to "urgent/stat" with clinical notes from provider
- Proactive communication wins: patients who are told proactively have 91% retention vs 54% when they discover the issue themselves
