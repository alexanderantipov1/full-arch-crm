# Treatment Decline

## Profile Summary
Patient who received a treatment plan but did not accept at presentation. Most common reason: cost shock without immediate financing solution. Second most common: fear/anxiety. Third: wants second opinion. Win-back window: 30–90 days.

## Conversion Intelligence
- **Converts when (re-engagement):** [[agents/FinancialCounselorAgent]] follow-up within 48h, phased plan offered by [[agents/TreatmentPlanAgent]], financing pre-qualification done proactively before follow-up call
- **Permanently lost when:** >90 days with no contact, bad review posted, went to competitor

## Re-engagement Sequence
1. Day 2: Financial counselor calls — lead with "I found a financing option for you"
2. Day 7: Provider sends brief personal video message via text
3. Day 21: SMS with phased plan option ("start with upper arch today")
4. Day 45: Final call — accept any partial treatment to rebuild relationship
